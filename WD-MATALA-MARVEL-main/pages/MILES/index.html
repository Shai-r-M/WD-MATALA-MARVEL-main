Spider-Man: Miles Morales Omnibus

Published:February 12, 2020

ON SALE FEBRUARY 2020. Welcome to the Marvel Universe, Miles Morales! Visionary writer Brian Michael Bendis completes his eighteen-year association with wall-crawlers by bringing the Ultimate Spider-Man to a brand-new life on a brand-new world — and not everything is how it was! Miles will have to find his feet quickly, though, when he goes up against one of the biggest bads in the Marvel Universe! Then, the villainous Black Cat plots to get her claws in this new Spidey — but will Miles find romance with the other-dimensional Spider-Gwen? And Spidey finds himself caught in the middle when Marvel’s heroes square off in a second Civil War! Plus: The secret history of Miles’ dad…Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D.! And a familiar face returns — along with an all-new, all-deadly Sinister Six! Collecting SPIDER-MAN (2016) #1-21 and #234-240, and SPIDER-GWEN (2015B) #16-18.
